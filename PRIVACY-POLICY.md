## 1. Introduction

PikMe ("App," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Location Information
- **GPS/Precise Location:** Used to find nearby restaurants (with your permission)
- **How it's used:** Restaurant recommendations, distance calculations
- **Storage:** Not permanently stored; used for current session only

### 2.2 User Profile Information
You voluntarily provide:
- Dietary preferences (vegetarian, vegan, halal, kosher, etc.)
- Health goals (weight loss, low sodium, high protein, etc.)
- Allergens/food restrictions
- Cuisine preferences
- Display name

**Storage:** Encrypted and stored in Supabase database

### 2.3 Usage Data
- Menu items viewed
- Items saved/bookmarked
- Chat interactions with Menu Mentor
- App features used
- Timestamps of usage

**Storage:** Stored for analytics and improving recommendations

### 2.4 Technical Information
- Device type and OS version
- App version
- IP address (via API logs)
- Device identifiers (anonymized)
