### 3. Allergen Warning ⚠️⚠️⚠️

**CRITICAL:** If you have food allergies:

#### DO NOT rely on PikMe alone
- AI analysis may miss allergens
- Cross-contamination not detected
- Ingredient changes without notice
- Hidden ingredients not identified

#### YOU MUST:
1. **Call restaurant directly** to confirm
2. **Ask about preparation methods** (shared equipment, utensils)
3. **Request ingredient lists** from chef/manager
4. **Verify with staff in person** if possible
5. **Always inform staff of severity** of your allergy

#### Life-Threatening Allergies:
- Do NOT risk using this app as sole reference
- Always speak to restaurant manager
- Request written ingredient confirmation
- Consider allergen-free restaurants only

### 7. Allergy Action Plan

**If you experience allergic reaction:**

1. **Immediate:** Seek emergency medical care
   - Call 911 (US) or emergency services
   - Tell them food allergen
   - Use EpiPen if available

2. **Within 24 Hours:**
   - Save packaging/receipt
   - Note time and symptoms
   - Take photos of menu

3. **Report To:**
   - Local health department
   - Restaurant manager
   - PikMe support (non-emergency)

4. **Follow Up:**
   - See doctor for evaluation
   - Get incident report from ER
   - Consider legal consultation if severe
